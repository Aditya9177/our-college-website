About KITS

Kakinada Institute of Technological Sciences (KITS), Ramachandrapuram was established in 2008. Run by Raja Ratna Healthcare and Educational Trust, it aims to provide technical education of excellence to the rural and semi-urban students of East Godavari, West Godavari, Visakhapatnam, and Srikakulam districts. The college is located 5 km from Ramachandrapuram.
